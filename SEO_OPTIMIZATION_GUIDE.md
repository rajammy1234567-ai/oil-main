## 📊 How to Speed Up Google Indexing

### Option 1: Google Search Console (Recommended)

1. **Sign in to Google Search Console**
   - Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
   - Add your website if not already added

2. **Request Indexing**
   - Click "URL Inspection" in the left sidebar
   - Enter: `https://karyor.com/product/karyor-mustard-oil-100-pure-cold-single-pressed-1l/`
   - Click "Request Indexing"
   - Repeat for: `https://karyor.com/product/karyo-mustard-oil-100-pure-cold-single-pressed-5l/`

3. **Submit Sitemap** (if you have one)
   - Go to "Sitemaps" in the left sidebar
   - Submit your sitemap URL
   - This helps Google find all your products automatically

### Option 2: Create a Sitemap

A sitemap helps Google discover all your products. You can:

1. **Manual Sitemap:** Create a `sitemap.xml` file listing all product URLs
2. **Django Sitemap:** Use Django's sitemap framework (I can help set this up)
3. **Third-party Tools:** Use online sitemap generators

### Option 3: Share on Social Media

- Share product links on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram
- Google crawls social media and will discover your links faster
- Use the product URLs directly in your posts
